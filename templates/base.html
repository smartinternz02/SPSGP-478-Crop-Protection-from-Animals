Protecting crops from intruding Animals

Animals should not be entered into the agricultural area

  

Problems with wild Animals

   

Wild animals are a special challenge for farmers throughout the world. Animals such as deer, wild boars, rabbits, moles, elephants, monkeys, and many others may cause serious damage to crops. They can damage the plants by feeding on plant parts or simply by running over the field and trampling over the crops.

  

Loss of crops due to Animal intrudance

   

  • Ultrasonic electronic repellent; silent to humans, high-frequency sound waves repel wild animals
  • Sonic electronic repellent; audible noise that scares animals
